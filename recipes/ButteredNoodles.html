Buttered Noodles Recipe

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Egg Noodles
  • Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Black Pepper

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat 2 quarts of water with 1 tablespoon kosher salt in a large pot on high heat until boiling. Add the egg noodles. Cook for 5 minutes. Reserve 1/4 cup of the pasta cooking liquid and drain the noodles.
  2. Place the drained hot noodles in a large bowl. Add the butter and Parmesan and stir until the butter has melted. Stir in a tablespoon or two of the pasta cooking water. Stir in the chopped parsley. Add salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
